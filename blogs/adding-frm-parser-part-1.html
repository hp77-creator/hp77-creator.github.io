Google Summer of Code is in motion, with this being a pen-ultimate week before mid evaluations, I am currently contributing in this project (https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/programs/2025/projects/vAVydntH).

In Community bonding period, members of MariaDB community had setup a call where we introduced ourselves and got to know about our mentors.
